    Adam Mayfield (born August 2, 1976) is an American actor, who has been portraying Scott Chandler on the ABC soap opera All My Children since April 2009. He was also seen in various other TV shows and movies guest-starring in minor co-starring roles. He attended the High School for the Performing Arts in Houston, Texas. He is a 2000 graduate with a BFA in Acting from the Depaul Theatre School then has appeared in numerous movies, television shows and commercials. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
