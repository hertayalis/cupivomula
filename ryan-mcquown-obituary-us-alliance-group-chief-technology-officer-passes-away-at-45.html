    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Rancho Santa Margarita, CA – The tech world and the US Alliance Group community are grieving the unexpected loss of their Chief Technology Officer, Ryan Mcquown. Ryan, a beloved figure, passed away on September 4, 2023, at the age of 45, leaving behind a legacy of dedication and warmth that touched the lives of many.

    US Alliance Group shared the heart-wrenching news, expressing deep sorrow at the sudden departure of their cherished colleague and friend. Ryan McQuown had been an integral part of the USAG team for an astounding 23 years, sharing his expertise and unwavering dedication. His passing has left an irreplaceable void in the hearts of his family, colleagues, and the entire USAG community.

    “Ryan’s untimely departure has left a profound void in the hearts of my family and the entire USAG team, with whom he shared his dedication and expertise for an incredible 23 years,” the organization said. His absence will be profoundly felt by all who had the privilege of knowing and working with him.

    Born on July 6th, 1978, Ryan was not just a Chief Technology Officer; he was a confidant and a genuine friend to many. Beyond his seemingly tough exterior, he possessed a heart that overflowed with kindness and empathy. Ryan was a man of larger-than-life character, known for his magnetic personality that effortlessly drew people to him.

    His laughter was infectious, bringing joy wherever it echoed, and his spirit was defined by boundless generosity. Ryan excelled at building deep and enduring connections, leaving an indelible mark on those fortunate enough to cross his path. He had an unparalleled willingness to extend a helping hand, often going to great lengths or even giving the shirt off his back to assist someone in need.

    Ryan Mcquown’s life serves as a testament to the extraordinary impact one person can have through warmth, compassion, and unwavering support for others. His legacy will continue to shine through the lives he touched and the memories he created.

    As the tech community and US Alliance Group grieve the loss of their beloved Chief Technology Officer, they join in offering thoughts and prayers to Ryan Mcquown’s family, friends, colleagues, and community during this difficult time.
