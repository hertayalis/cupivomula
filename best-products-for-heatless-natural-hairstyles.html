    The 5 Best Products For Heatless Styling

    25 May 2024, 00:00

    Seasonal changes can be challenging for textured hair. And we all know that not all products are created equal, so determining what works best to produce a look without using any heat is even more difficult.

    Although many naturals avoid using hot tools on their hair to prevent damage, understanding what steps they need to take to prep and protect their hair is just as important. The first step, according to Jennifer Lord, hair designer and author of Natural Hollywood, is to properly wash and condition your hair.

    "I recommend using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner," says the expert. "Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. Then style the hair with an anti-frizz, moisturizer, or anti-humectant product."

    While keeping heat to a minimum is the healthier option for textured hair, one of the most common concerns about going heatless is the possibility of frizz. To combat this, Lord recommends applying the product to the entire hair shaft and using your fingers to achieve the desired style while holding the hair tight. For natural hairstyles, she recommends detangling thoroughly and performing a refined comb out. "After using a moisturizing and anti-frizz product, allow the style to settle for the night," she says."The next day, play with the style to achieve the desired look."

    Ahead, we share five of the best products to help you get just that.

    Alikay Naturals Totally Twisted Loc Butter

    If you want a product with no buildup, flakes, or clogged pores, you're in luck, 'cause this is your new go-to option. This product is made with natural ingredients such as cocoa butter, vitamin E, and grape seed oil — plus it smells like cucumber melon. It can be used on loose hair for curling and styling, as well as locs and twists.

    Mixed Chicks Coil Kink & Curl Styling Cream

    Six nourishing oils are used in this curl cream to assist hair retaining moisture while allowing for simple shaping. Excellent for curls, two-strand twists, bantu knots, and any other natural hair styling techniques. This is a great option for thicker, fuller textures.

    Mielle Pomegranate and Honey Twisting Souffle

    A type 4 hair product that is lightweight and precisely developed? Doesn't get any better than that. This soufflé provides semi-deep conditioning, while defining, protecting, and moisturizing. Did we mention it's also free of hazardous chemicals and preservatives?

    The moisture miracle series of products was designed specifically for coily and curly textures. Free of parabens, mineral oil, sulfates, and petrolatum, as well as other potentially dangerous ingredients. It's enriched with shea butter, which hydrates, strengthens, and protects hair, while the flaxseed oil adds natural hold and shine.

    Shedavi Crowned Styling Wash & Go Gel

    With this product, you'll get the ultimate post-show finish! This gel is fantastic for clumping curls and giving a beautiful, well-defined wash-and-go look. It's vegan, cruelty-free, paraben-free, and sulfate-free.
