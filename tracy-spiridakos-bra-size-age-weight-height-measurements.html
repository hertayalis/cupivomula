    Biography:

    Tracy Spiridakos was born on February 20, 1988 in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. She started acting in junior high school and studied at the Actors Training Centre of Manitoba.

    In 2007, she moved to Vancouver to pursue acting. She made her first television role in the series Supernatural. She then continued working in television in such shows as Bionic Woman, The L World, Hellcats and Psych.

    She won her first starring role in the Canadian Teletoon series Majority Rules! in 2009, and made her feature film debut in Rise of the Planet of the Apes.

    As of 2012, she plays the role of Charlotte Matheson in Revolution.
