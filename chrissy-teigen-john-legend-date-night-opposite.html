    Chrissy Teigen and John Legend are one of our absolute favorite star couples. How could they not be?

    They post hilarious Instagrams, they have the cutest couple moments, they have an adorable 1-year-old Luna, and, of course, they have killer style, which they demonstrated yet again on Thursday night.

    The duo stepped out for the Los Angeles opening night of Turn Me Loose, and while they both looked spectacular.

    Teigen opted for a blush pink dress with soft feather detailing and a wrap-style slit that showed off her leg. She kept her makeup neutral and paired the dress with simple Hearts On Fire teardrop earrings.

    Legend, on the other hand, went super bold with his look. He wore a patterned suit with burgundy trim and matching loafers.

    Even though Teigen's dress had a classic vibe and Legend's was super trendy, they still complimented each other on the red carpet. But let's be honest, with this star couple, they could probably wear anything and still be in sync.
