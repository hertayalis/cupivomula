    I Know I Love You Chinese Drama Story

    The story of the Chinese drama I Know I Love You revolves around Xu Nuo whose family wants her to get married. She is unlucky in finding a desired partner for her. She meets Zhao Yin who is a vet. He is also busy fulfilling the responsibilities of his family.

    Xu Nuo and Zhao Yin find each other as the perfect partner but they hesitate to start any romantic relationship. They are not sure whether they are good friends or more than that. Eventually, They accept their love for each other.
