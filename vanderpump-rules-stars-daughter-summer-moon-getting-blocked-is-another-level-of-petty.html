    Vanderpump Rules star Scheana Shay’s child, Summer Moon, was blocked by Tom Sandoval during scandal and grief, after his friend Ali died. Fans call the move “petty” – but he did unblock her.

    After Scandoval went public, Tom Sandoval was feeling really low. So when he saw Scheana Shay making a podcast, he decided to block everyone associated with her, including her innocent daughter.

    Who is Summer Moon on Vanderpump?

    Summer Moon Honey Davies is Vanderpump Rules star Scheana Shay’s two-year-old daughter. With 143K followers on Instagram, she’s already a social media sensation before she’s even reached three!

    Scheana and Brock welcomed Summer Moon in April 2021, more than a year before their extravagant wedding in Mexico. Since then, she’s become passionate about ballet and making appearances on Bravo.

    In 2023, Summer broke her forearm but has since healed. She’s a warrior who loves sports and Disney princesses, having enjoyed a game day for the L.A. Chargers and paying princess Belle a visit last year!

    Scheana Shay’s daughter was blocked by Sandoval

    Scheana Shay’s two-year-old daughter, Summer Moon, which is technically the Bravo star’s second account, was blocked by Sandoval. He claimed he had blocked everyone associated with her.

    Everyone but Scheana’s mother was blocked, including her husband Brock Davies and young daughter. Tom Schwartz urged his best friend to unblock the toddler, to which Sandoval claimed he already had.

    Sandoval explained himself on the Vanderpump Rules After Show. He said: “When I do, I hit block account or accounts associated with them. For some reason, that blocked Summer Moon’s account.”

    ‘Another level of petty’

    When Pump Rules fans found out Sandoval had literally blocked a toddler, they couldn’t help but laugh. Many have taken to social media to share their thoughts on the news…

    One wrote: “Blocking Summer Moon is so wildly petty that it’s actually peak humour #PumpRules.” Another penned: “YOU CANT BLOCK SUMMER MOON!! #VPR #PumpRules.”

    “As if Tom SCUMdoval wasn’t bad enough, he reached a new low by blocking baby Summer Moon on social media. How. Dare. He,” reacted a Pump Rules viewer.
